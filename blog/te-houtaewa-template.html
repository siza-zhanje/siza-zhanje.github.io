My name is Siza Zhanje, actually Sizalobuhle Zhanje. It means "Bringing Beauty". I was born in Zimbabwe many mooooooons ago so I feel quite ancient amongst you all, but left in when I was 20 to travel the world. My husband and I moved to New Zealand 19 and half years ago with our 6 month old son. We now have a daughter too and neither speak Ndebele or Shona! It makes for interesting visits when we go back to see our families.
